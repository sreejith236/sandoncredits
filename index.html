Vision

Our vision is to provide accessible and affordable credit to the people of Tamil Nadu, particularly those who are underbanked or underserved by traditional financial institutions. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to improve their lives and their communities through access to capital, and we are committed to providing this access in a fair and transparent way.

Mission

Our mission is to create a strong and sustainable community of members who can support each other financially, and to promote economic growth and development in the region. We believe that by pooling our resources and sharing the risks and benefits of lending, we can create a more inclusive and equitable financial system that works for everyone.

Why Choose us

We can provide you with accessible and affordable credit, flexible loan terms, competitive rates and fees, financial education and counseling, community-focused support, transparent and fair financial services, and a social impact.

  • Accessible and Affordable Credit: We believe in providing accessible and affordable credit to everyone, regardless of their income level or background. Our lending decisions are based on your creditworthiness and ability to repay, rather than on collateral or other assets.
  • Flexibility: We offer flexible loan terms that can be customized to meet your individual needs. Whether you need a short-term loan or a long-term investment, we can help you find the right solution.
  • Competitive Rates and Fees: Our interest rates and fees are competitive with other financial institutions, making our loans an affordable option for many people.

FAQ

  • What is a Private Credit Society?

    A Private Credit Society is a member-owned financial institution that provides banking services, including savings accounts, fixed deposit accounts, Investments, Insurance and loans. It is governed by its members, who elect a board of directors to manage the organization.

  • How do I become a member of the Private Credit Society?

    To become a member, you will need to visit one of our branches and complete an application form. You will also need to provide identification documents and make an initial deposit.

  • What are the benefits of becoming a member?

    As a member, you will have access to a range of financial services, including savings accounts, fixed deposit accounts,Investments, Insurance and loans. You will also have a say in how the organization is run, as members elect the board of directors.

  • What types of loans do you offer?

    We offer a range of loans to our members, including personal loans, business loans, and agriculture loans. Our loans are designed to meet the unique needs of our members and are offered at competitive interest rates.

  • How do I apply for a loan?

    You can apply for a loan by visiting one of our branches and speaking with a loan officer. You will need to provide documentation to support your loan application, such as income statements and credit reports.

  • Is my money safe with the Private Credit Society?

    We take the security of your funds seriously and have measures in place to ensure the safety of your deposits. Our deposit accounts are also covered by the Deposit Insurance and Credit Guarantee Corporation (DICGC), which provides insurance on your deposits up to Rs. 5 lakh per depositor per bank.
